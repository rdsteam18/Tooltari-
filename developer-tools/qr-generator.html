QR Code Generator

Convert links, contact details, or credentials into a printable QR code immediately.

100% Client-Side Vectors
The QR image code compiles inside your browser memory cache. None of your URL strings or inputs are uploaded to servers.

How to Generate a QR Code

  1. Type or paste your link or text parameters into the Text or URL Link field.
  2. Click on Generate QR Code Now. ToolTari draws the graphic instantly.
  3. View the generated code layout in the center canvas container.
  4. Click on Save QR Image to save the high-resolution PNG on your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

What occurs if my text contains Unicode or non-English characters?

Our generator translates string sets into standard UTF-8 indices before compiling vectors, allowing you to convert messages, links, and passwords in any language perfectly.

Is this secure?

Yes. Because the graphic compiles local pixel arrays in-browser, no information is leaked onto servers.
