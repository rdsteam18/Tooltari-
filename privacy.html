Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

At ToolTari, accessible from tooltari.netlify.app, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document outlines how visitor statistics are managed and details our absolute local processing system.

1. 100% In-Browser Local Processing

Unlike standard PDF and image converter sites, ToolTari does not upload, copy, transfer, or process your files on cloud servers. All logic is loaded directly into your local browser cache. Calculations are executed client-side in sandboxed RAM. Consequently:

  • Your private contracts, invoices, snapshots, and text files never leave your computer.
  • We do not (and cannot) view, store, index, or distribute any document you upload to our dashboard.
  • All processing is subject only to your browser’s sandboxed security policies.

2. Google AdSense Cookie Policies

We use third-party advertising companies, specifically Google AdSense, to serve ads when you visit our website. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on user visits to this site and other sites on the Internet. These cookies enable Google and its partners to serve ads based on your visit history.

You can choose to decline personalized advertising by visiting Google’s Ad Settings page. Alternatively, you can block third-party cookies inside your browser preferences.

3. Log Files and Analytics

ToolTari follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks. This data is not linked to any personally identifiable information and is used solely to analyze web traffic trends.

4. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its Terms of Service.
