How to Rotate PDF pages online

  1. Upload your document by clicking Select File or dragging it in.
  2. Choose your rotation angle: 90° Clockwise, 180°, or 90° Counter-Clockwise.
  3. Click on Rotate PDF Now. ToolTari updates page catalog vectors.
  4. Download your rotated document.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will this change the layout elements of my file?

No. This operation simply modifies the internal page rotation parameter dictionary block. It does not compress, re-arrange, or alter structural vectors of your pages.

Does this work with scanned documents?

Yes. It works perfectly with scanned sheets, contract agreements, and book slides.
