How to delete pages from PDF documents

  1. Drag your document into the drop zone or choose Select File.
  2. Specify the exact page numbers you want to remove (e.g. 2, 4).
  3. Click on Delete Pages Now. ToolTari deletes target elements.
  4. Download the new PDF with deleted pages omitted.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I delete multiple pages at once?

Yes. Just list the page numbers separated by commas. For example: 1, 3, 5 will remove pages 1, 3, and 5 from your document.

Are my documents secure?

Yes, all editing processes run locally on your browser. Your files are never uploaded.
