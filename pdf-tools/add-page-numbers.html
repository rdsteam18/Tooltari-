How to add page numbers to PDF documents

  1. Upload your document by clicking Select File or dragging it in.
  2. Choose your numbering alignment: Bottom Center or Top Center.
  3. Click on Add Page Numbers Now. ToolTari overlays the text vectors.
  4. Download the completed PDF.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will it overwrite text that is already in my headers or footers?

Our tool draws pagination text at a set coordinate margin (30px from bottom/top). We recommend checking that your margins are clear to prevent overlay text clashes.

Is this file-safe?

Yes. All operations run locally inside your browser window cache. Your file remains 100% confidential.
