How to Rotate Images online

  1. Drag your image into the dropzone or choose Select Image.
  2. Choose your rotation angle: 90° Clockwise, 180°, or 270°.
  3. Click on Rotate Image Now. ToolTari translates canvas buffers locally.
  4. Save the rotated image.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does rotation affect photo dimensions?

Yes. Rotating by 90° or 270° swaps the width and height dimensions of your image. For example, a portrait image of 800x1200 will be exported as a landscape image of 1200x800. Flipped (180°) images preserve original dimension sizes.

Is this file-safe?

Yes. All operations execute locally in browser memory. No data leaves your machine.
