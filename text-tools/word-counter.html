How to count words and characters online

  1. Type directly or paste copy text from your editor into the Input Text Data box.
  2. As you type, ToolTari parses the text sequences and updates the Statistics Dashboard in real time.
  3. Read metrics for words, spaces, paragraph breaks, and reading speed.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is the reading speed calculated?

The reading time is calculated based on the average silent reading speed of adults, which is approximately 200 words per minute (WPM).

Is there an input length restriction?

No, there are no length restrictions. The script easily processes strings containing hundreds of thousands of words instantly in your browser memory.
